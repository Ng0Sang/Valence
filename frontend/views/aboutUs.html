About Us

Valence was founded in the year 2023, with Sang David as CEO
and Founder. Valence is sponsored by big organizations round
the world. Like, Google, JBL and VS. We have collaborators who
work with Doctors to issue Kits and Drugs to users who need it
at Home. Large Pharmaceutical Industries in Nigeria whose aim
is to make life easy are part of the collaborators. We provide ex

to any of them for help when you need it.
